Agenda

Create, manage, and share to-do lists with ease when using Agenda. Our web-based application allows you to manage multiple to-do lists on your personal dashboard or collaborate with other users through our common boards. To begin, create a free account.

To-Do Lists

Our to-do lists follow the familiar Kanban layout used by multiple planning softwares including Jira™. Lists are represented by columns which contain the tasks. Task information may include a title, description, and due date. Tasks that are over due or due today are easily identifiable by their colors which dynamically change depending on their status. Red tasks are overdue, blue tasks are due today, grey tasks are due in the future or are not assigned a due date, and green tasks are completed. Tasks are also automatically ordered to show the most pressing tasks first. New lists are easily added and removed.
